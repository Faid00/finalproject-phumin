Bachelor Programs

Information Technology (IT)

Focuses on systems administration, networking, databases, and the implementation of information systems in organizations.

Computer Science (CS)

Covers programming, algorithms, software development, and theoretical foundations of computing systems.

Electronics and Communication

Combines electronic engineering and communication technologies including signal processing, circuits, and wireless systems.

Graphic Design and Multimedia

Focuses on visual communication, digital media, animation, branding, and multimedia production using industry tools.

Business Management and IT

Integrates core business knowledge with IT, emphasizing digital management, business analytics, and e-commerce systems.

Network Engineering and Cyber Security

Prepares students to design, manage, and secure networks with a strong focus on ethical hacking and defense systems.

Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

Concentrates on data analytics, machine learning, AI systems development, and practical applications in various sectors.
